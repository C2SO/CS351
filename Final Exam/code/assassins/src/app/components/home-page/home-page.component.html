Cloud Assasin Rules

To play Assassin, click the 'Join Game' button below to be addes to the cloud database. Once there are enough players in the system, the Moderator will start a game. If you are a participant in the game of Assassin, you will recive an automated email with your current target. If you should succede to kill your target, you will then need to report the kill to the Moderator. Once the Moderator has confirmed your kill, the Moderator will update the database and you will recive a new email with a new target. Once there is only one Assassin remaining, that Assassin is the winner.
